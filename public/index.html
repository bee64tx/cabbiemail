Cabbiemail: Point-to-Point Transit

Our Philosophy

Cabbiemail is a straightforward proposition: an email service built on the foundational principles of a proper taxi ride. You get in, we slide the glass partition shut, and we take your messages exactly where they need to go.

There are no sponsored detours, no third parties squeezing into the passenger seat, and we certainly don't rifle through the boot to see what you're carrying. We don't track you, and we don't monetise your metadata. It is just clean, unbothered, point-to-point transit.

The 'For Hire' Light is Off

Cabbiemail was cobbled together by a few volunteers who simply wanted a quiet, private place to park their digital lives. It is kept on the road entirely by a friendly whip-round among our existing users to keep the servers ticking over.

The service works brilliantly, but we have absolutely no intention of scaling it up. Turning this into a massive public platform would mean dealing with support tickets, drafting endless terms of service, and, worst of all, interacting with the general public. Frankly, that sounds like a tremendous amount of faff.

Consequently, we aren't taking anyone off the rank. The registration ledger is locked in the glovebox, and we do not maintain a waiting list. We have enough passengers, the meter is off, and we are perfectly happy driving with the doors locked. If you already have an account, in you get.
